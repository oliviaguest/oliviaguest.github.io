About

Hi! I am an Assistant Professor of Computational Cognitive Science. I work in the Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence department in the Donders Centre for Cognition and the School of Artificial Intelligence at Radboud University in the Netherlands.

My research interests comprise (meta)theoretical, critical, and radical perspectives on the neuro-, computational, and cognitive sciences broadly construed. Additionally, I am an Associate Editor-in-Chief for ReScience C and for the Journal of Open Source Software.
